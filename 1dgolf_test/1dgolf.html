HOW TO PLAY
⛳ BASICS

Select a club and press the SHOOT button to start the gauge. Release/stop to fire the ball. Higher gauge = more distance.

📱 Mobile
Tap SHOOT → tap ■STOP to fire
🖥️ PC
Hold SHOOT → release to fire
💨 WAIT FOR WIND

Press WAIT n to change the wind value. + extends distance, − shortens it. Usable n times.

🏌️ TERRAIN
  • ROUGH: Distance reduced
  • BUNKER: Distance heavily reduced. Irons (5I/8I) only
  • WATER / OB: +1 stroke penalty, replay from previous position
  • GREEN: Use putter (PT). Slope ±0 guide is shown
✨ SKILLS
  • Power Shot: Gauge cap raised to 120%
  • Terrain Ignore: Fairway distance even on ROUGH/BUNKER
  • Retry Shot: Reshoot from previous position without adding a stroke
  • Wind/Slope Cancel: Sets wind and slope to ±0
  • Start Over: Restart the hole from the beginning
🏆 SCORE & POINTS

Points (pts) decrease per shot and are awarded on hole clear. Give up at par+4 strokes or more. After each hole, visit the SHOP to upgrade clubs or increase skill uses.

⚔ VS MODE

Championship course (9H) only. After Player 1 plays a hole, the CPU plays the same hole. Only Player 1 can upgrade in the SHOP.
